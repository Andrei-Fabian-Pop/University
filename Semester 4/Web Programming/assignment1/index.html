Breakfast

Egg and Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 avocado
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 lemon
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread
  2. Mash the avocado with a fork, add lemon juice, salt and pepper
  3. Heat olive oil in a non-stick pan
  4. Crack eggs into the pan and cook until the whites are set and the yolks are still runny
  5. Spread the mashed avocado on the toast and place the eggs on top

Lunch

Greek Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 head of romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 1/2 red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup kalamata olives
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste


Instructions:

  1. Wash and chop the lettuce, onion, cucumber and bell pepper
  2. Halve the cherry tomatoes and kalamata olives
  3. Combine all the vegetables in a bowl
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and red wine vinegar, and season with salt and pepper
  5. Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese on top and serve
